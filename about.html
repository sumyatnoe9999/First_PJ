'Fresh' Organic Shop & Farming Myanmar

We are varieties of organic fruits and vegetables shop in Pathein, Myanmar. We grow a range of greens and salad mixes while training a young team in organic farming and good business practices. We are a employee-owned business.
We are also proud to work with a wonderful community of local producers and offer a diverse range of their products on our online shop and to create opportunities consumers to buy local products from farmers directly.
